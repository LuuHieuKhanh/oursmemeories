Design ONLY the "Graduation Wall" section for a premium digital graduation memory website.

Do NOT design any other section.

This section celebrates the graduation of five university friends.

Each person graduated on a different day.

Do NOT create a timeline.

Do NOT imply that everyone graduated together.

The design should feel elegant, clean and proud.

Think of it as a premium graduation exhibition wall.

==================================================
STYLE
==================================================

Follow the established Design System.

Visual references:

• Apple editorial layout
• Premium graduation photobook
• Museum exhibition
• Minimal gallery

Avoid:

• Timeline
• Masonry gallery
• Scrapbook style
• Social media feed
• Dashboard
• Heavy decorations

This section should feel calmer and more mature than previous sections.

==================================================
BACKGROUND
==================================================

Background:

Warm White (#FFFDF9)

Very subtle paper texture.

A few soft golden sunlight highlights.

No colorful decorations.

No doodles.

Let the graduation photos become the focus.

==================================================
SECTION HEADER
==================================================

Centered.

Large title:

Graduation Wall

Subtitle:

Different days.

The same destination.

Large whitespace.

==================================================
MAIN LAYOUT
==================================================

Display five graduation portraits.

Arrange them horizontally.

Each portrait should have generous spacing.

Do NOT overlap the photos.

Each photo should feel equally important.

The layout should resemble an art gallery exhibition.

==================================================
PHOTO CARD
==================================================

Each graduation photo is displayed inside
a premium white frame.

Rounded corners (24px).

Soft shadow.

Wide white border around the image.

The frame resembles a luxury printed photograph.

Below each photo:

Student name

Graduation date

Example:

Khánh

June 28, 2026

Typography should remain clean and minimal.

==================================================
PHOTO INTERACTION
==================================================

Hover:

Photo lifts slightly.

Scale to 1.03.

Shadow increases softly.

Background behind the selected photo
becomes subtly brighter.

==================================================
CLICK
==================================================

Clicking a photo opens
a fullscreen photo viewer.

The photo becomes much larger.

Display:

Large graduation portrait

Student name

Graduation date

Optional caption

Example:

"The end of one journey.
The beginning of another."

Provide Previous / Next navigation.

==================================================
BACKGROUND DECORATION
==================================================

Behind the photos,

add extremely subtle abstract shapes.

Examples:

soft circles

light paper cut shapes

very faint graduation cap outlines

Keep opacity below 5%.

The background should never distract
from the portraits.

==================================================
ANIMATIONS
==================================================

Section enters using fade-up.

Photos appear one after another.

Hover:

Lift

Scale

Shadow

Click:

Photo expands smoothly.

Backdrop fades in.

==================================================
RESPONSIVE
==================================================

Desktop:

Five photos in one row.

Tablet:

Three photos on first row.

Two photos on second row.

Mobile:

Horizontal swipe gallery.

Large cards.

==================================================
EMOTIONAL GOAL
==================================================

This section should make visitors feel proud.

Every graduation portrait should feel like
a milestone worth celebrating.

Although the graduation dates are different,
the design should communicate that
all five friends successfully reached
the same destination.
