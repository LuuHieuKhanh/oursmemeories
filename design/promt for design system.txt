# Design System Specification

## Premium Digital Graduation Album

Design a premium digital graduation album for five university friends.

The website is designed to preserve memories, not to function as a social network.

The design language should feel timeless so that it still looks beautiful after 10 years.

The overall atmosphere should resemble:

• Premium editorial magazine
• Korean graduation photobook
• Handmade scrapbook
• Apple Human Interface spacing
• MUJI minimalism

Avoid anything that looks like:

• Dashboard
• Material Design
• Facebook
• Instagram
• Pinterest
• Notion
• Gaming website
• Neon UI
• Heavy Glassmorphism
• Heavy Gradients
• Corporate Landing Page

---

# DESIGN PRINCIPLE

Every page should feel like a page inside a printed graduation album.

Users should forget they are browsing a website.

Instead,

they should feel they are slowly turning pages of memories.

Images are always the visual focus.

The interface exists only to support the memories.

PHOTO STYLE

Photos should always be the strongest visual element.

Use

Polaroid frames

Instant film frames

Rounded rectangle frames

Paper borders

Never crop faces aggressively.

ILLUSTRATION STYLE

Very limited.

Soft.

Hand-drawn.

---

ANIMATION

Animation should feel alive,

not playful.

Use

Fade

Float

Slide

Scale

Small rotation

Animation duration

400-700ms

Floating elements

8-12 seconds loop

Never bounce.

Never shake.

---

SCROLL EXPERIENCE

One long storytelling page.

Each section almost fills one screen.

Large transitions.

Images appear using fade-up.

Text appears slightly later.

This creates cinematic storytelling.

---

---

RESPONSIVE

Desktop First.

Tablet.

Mobile.

Maintain generous whitespace.

Never reduce spacing too much.

---

EMOTIONAL GOAL

Every visitor should feel

"I miss those days."

rather than

"This is a beautiful website."

The memories should always be more important than the interface.
