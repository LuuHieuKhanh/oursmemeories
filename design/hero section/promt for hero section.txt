Design ONLY the Hero section of a premium digital graduation memory website.

Do NOT design the rest of the page.

The Hero should occupy exactly one full viewport (100vh).

The Hero is the cover page of a digital graduation album.

Users should immediately feel:
"This isn't a website."
"This is the first page of our memory album."

====================================================

Apple-level spacing

Do NOT make it look like a birthday invitation.

Do NOT use cartoon balloons.

====================================================
LAYOUT
====================================================

There is NO navigation bar.

No logo.

No menu.

No buttons.

The Hero should rely entirely on composition.

The composition consists of three layers:

Layer 1

Sky background

Layer 2

The rope and balloons

Layer 3

Title and subtitle

====================================================
THE ROPE
====================================================

Place one natural cotton rope

stretching horizontally

across around 70% of the page width.

The rope should NOT be perfectly straight.

It has a natural curve.

The rope should look real.

Not illustrated.

Not cartoon.

====================================================
BALLOONS
====================================================

Five transparent balloons

hang from the rope.

Each balloon hangs independently.

Every balloon has

slightly different height

slightly different size

slightly different rotation

The arrangement should feel natural.

The balloons should form an organic composition.

NOT perfectly aligned.

====================================================
BALLOON MATERIAL
====================================================

Transparent glass balloon.

Soft reflection.

Very subtle highlight.

Tiny shadow.

No saturated colors.

No rainbow reflections.

Minimal.

Elegant.

====================================================
INSIDE EACH BALLOON
====================================================

Inside every balloon

place one circular portrait.

The portrait fills around 80% of the balloon.

Leave transparent margin.

Portrait border

Thin white border.

Very soft shadow.

====================================================
BALLOON STRINGS
====================================================

Each balloon string

is thin

slightly curved

different length

The balloons should gently float.
