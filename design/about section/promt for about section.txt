Design ONLY the "Meet The Five" section for a premium digital graduation memory website.

Do NOT design the Hero or other sections.

This section introduces the five main characters before the story continues.

The concept is inspired by university student ID cards.

The atmosphere should feel elegant, warm, interactive and nostalgic.

==================================================
STYLE
==================================================

Use the existing Design System.

Style references:

• Premium editorial
• Korean graduation photobook
• Apple Human Interface spacing
• Soft scrapbook aesthetic

Avoid:

• Dashboard UI
• Social media cards
• Material Design
• Heavy shadows
• Neon colors
• Corporate feeling

==================================================
BACKGROUND
==================================================

Background color:

Warm White (#FFFDF9)

Very subtle paper texture.

Small hand-drawn doodles in the corners.

Examples:

tiny stars

paper airplanes

sparkles

small flowers

Everything should remain subtle.

==================================================
SECTION HEADER
==================================================

Large title:

Meet The Five

Subtitle:

Five different personalities.
One unforgettable journey.

Center aligned.

Large whitespace above and below.

==================================================
MAIN LAYOUT
==================================================

The entire section focuses on five student ID cards.

There should be NO text below the cards except a small instruction.

The cards are arranged horizontally.

The cards overlap each other slightly.

Each card overlaps around 20–25%.

The center card is slightly higher than the others.

The composition should resemble someone placing five student ID cards on a desk.

Do NOT align them perfectly.

Give each card a tiny rotation between -3° and +3°.

The layout should feel natural.

==================================================
CARD DESIGN
==================================================

Each card is inspired by a real university student ID card.

Rounded corners (24px).

White plastic material.

Soft shadow.

Small punched hole at the top center.

A pastel colored strip at the bottom.

Each person has a different accent color.

Example:

Baby Blue

Peach

Mint

Lavender

Butter

Each card only displays:

Portrait photo

Person's name

Nothing else.

Do NOT show faculty.

Do NOT show nickname.

Do NOT show course.

Keep the front of the card extremely clean.

==================================================
HOVER INTERACTION
==================================================

When hovering over a card:

The selected card lifts upward around 16px.

Scale to 1.05.

Shadow becomes slightly stronger.

The card rotates back to 0°.

Neighboring cards move slightly sideways to create space.

The hovered card always appears above all other cards.

Transition:

350ms ease.

The interaction should feel similar to selecting cards in a card game.

==================================================
CLICK INTERACTION
==================================================

Clicking a card opens a large centered modal.

Background becomes softly blurred.

The modal fades in.

Scale animation from 0.95 to 1.

==================================================
MODAL DESIGN
==================================================

The modal itself should look like an enlarged premium student ID card.

Maximum width around 900px.

Rounded corners (32px).

Soft shadow.

White background.

Subtle paper texture.

==================================================
MODAL LAYOUT
==================================================

Split into two columns.

LEFT COLUMN

Large portrait photo.

Student ID design.

Pastel accent strip at the bottom.

Small decorative university stamp.

Small text:

MEMORIES COLLECTION

2022–2026

RIGHT COLUMN

Large handwritten name.

Faculty

Course

Academic Year

Example:

Large spacing.

==================================================
EMOTIONAL GOAL
==================================================

Users should feel like they are picking up and reading a real student ID card from university.

The interaction should feel personal.

Before seeing any memories,

users should first get to know the five friends.

The section should encourage curiosity and emotional connection before continuing the story.
